# Terraria RPG Mod: Ascend Your Potential

Transform your Terraria experience into a full-fledged RPG adventure. This mod adds a deep leveling system, tiered job classes, custom skills, and dynamic world scaling that keeps the game challenging from Green Slimes to the Moon Lord.

Inspired by classic RPGs, you can build a tanky Knight, a devastating Mage, or a tactical Sniper‚Äîand swap stat builds freely to fit your playstyle.

## üõ°Ô∏è Class & Job System
Start as a **Novice** and advance through tiers:
- **Tier 1 (Lv 10+):** Warrior, Ranger, Mage, Summoner.
- **Tier 2 (Lv 50+):** Knight, Berserker, Sniper, Sorcerer, Necromancer, and more.
- **Tier 3 (Endgame):** Final advancements (Coming Soon).

Visit the **RPG Merchant** (spawns after Level 10) for Job Advancement Scrolls and skill books.

## üìä Deep Stat Customization
Build with a **12-Stat System**:
- **Core:** Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, Focus.
- **Survival:** Vitality (HP), Defense, Fortitude (Status Resist).
- **Utility:** Agility (Speed/Dodge), Wisdom (Mana), Stamina, Luck, Rogue.

Each level grants **Stat Points** plus **Auto-Growth** based on your class so you scale naturally while still steering your build.

## ‚öîÔ∏è Skills & Combat
- **Active Skills:** New skill hotbar for offensive/buff skills.
- **Dash System:** Dedicated dash (Default: F) using Stamina with i-frames.
- **Sweet Spot XP:** Bonus XP for fighting near-level enemies; chain kills for combo multipliers.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Dynamic World Scaling
- **World Level:** Rises as you defeat bosses; keeps progression meaningful.
- **Monster & Projectile Scaling:** Enemies and their projectiles scale by world level and biome difficulty.
- **Mod Compatibility:** Designed to pair with big content mods (Calamity, Thorium, etc.).

## üñ•Ô∏è Modern UI
- **RPG HUD:** HP/Mana/Stamina/XP bars with clean readability.
- **Character Menu:** Press **C** (default) to manage stats, job info, and skills.
- **Floating Combat Text:** XP gain, crits, and elemental feedback.

## üéÆ How to Start
1. Create a Level 1 Novice.
2. Fight to gain XP (best rewards from near-level enemies).
3. Allocate Stat Points via the Menu key.
4. At Level 10, let the **RPG Merchant** move in and buy a "First Job Scroll."
